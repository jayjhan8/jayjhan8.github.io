2018-05-17

I’m home!! Sorry it’s taken a bit since I’ve last posted. I am much better now, but I struggled alot this time around. I felt like a tired runner crawling to the finish line. I couldn’t really eat much at all. I usually weigh around 130 lbs, and I was probably closer to 135lbs before I started chemo, but this recent cycle set me back to about 115lbs. Thankfully, I’m back to 125lbs today, but I just couldn’t keep any food down my throat this time. Luckily, I didn’t vomit, and I made it a personal goal not to do so even though there were couple times I came pretty close to it. Albeit my tastebuds going bitter, I am now eating much better and actually finishing meals. I am going to focus on rebuilding myself over these next weeks – eating healthier and exercising regularly. The next scan is scheduled to take place on June 25th, and my doc will go over the results with me the week afterwards. Let’s continually hope for good news!
