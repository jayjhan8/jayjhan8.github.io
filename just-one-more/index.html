Just one more

2018-05-07

Good news. All around. My doc came in and started with “Let me tell you the good news first…” but he said that was it. Only good news. Really good news.

The questionable matter in liver is not cancer. It’s still the same size as it was before, which means that it doesn’t respond to chemo and therefore is benign. The tumors in my lung have shrunk significantly. I had two big ones approximately about 1.5cm each, one is now at 5mm, and the other at 8mm. Doc had mentioned potential surgery, but now he even thinks that’s unnecessary. So it’s this cycle of chemo, and I have one more CT scan to verify that both lesions have reduced beyond the need of surgery.

I’m going on my first bag of chemo in a few minutes, and I feel the impending nausea. But who cares, THIS IS MY LAST ONE!
