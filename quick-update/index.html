Quick update

2018-04-29

I realized that I didn’t speak about couple big events coming up. I have finished 2 rounds of chemo and I’m currently on my last off week before I head back into the hospital. This coming Thursday, I have my 2nd CT scan. This is the first since I’ve gotten chemo, so we’ll have a good comparison of before and after chemo. Although I won’t know much about it that day, I continually ask for your thoughts and prayers that the cancer is full eradicated. I’m nervous, but I hope for great results. I go see my oncologist the Monday I get admitted for my 3rd cycle. This is the meeting I find out about a lot of things. Cancer in my liver, how much the tumors in the lungs have shrunk, etc. It’s been tough, but I’ve been preparing myself for 4 cycles of chemo. It could be more, or less. But I’m thinking about one project at a time, and I can only hope and pray that the doctor has only good things to say. We’ve had an amazing week of sun here in Seattle with a quick rain break this weekend. I’m looking forward to these brighters days!
